Under the Hood

ReAct AI Orchestrator

A 5-agent orchestrator (Scanner, Screener, Risk, Execution, Reflection) that manages continuous learning and decision flow.

6-Gate Risk Framework

Robust safety checks before execution: time, cooldown, feedback, conviction, loss cap, and drawdown floor.

Live Multi-Source Crawlers

Data crawlers across multiple platforms for sentiment, fundamentals, and market context.

Multi-Factor Conviction Score

A transparent scoring model combining sentiment, technicals, momentum, fundamentals, and analyst signals.

Optional Execution Automation

Support for workflow automation with risk controls and monitoring during market hours.
