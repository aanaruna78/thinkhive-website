What Protecto does for you

Protecto quietly runs in the background and watches for phone-level risk signals: spam patterns, fraud numbers, data breach activity and SIM changes.

Spam & fraud call guard Data breach alerts SIM swap early warning Risky app hints
  • • India-specific spam & fraud number reputation with community feedback.
  • • Alerts when your identifiers appear in breach dumps or leak datasets.
  • • Early warning for SIM change & port-out patterns (where supported).
  • • Guidance on apps that over-request permissions or behave suspiciously.
